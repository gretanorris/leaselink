Include a "Credit-Worthiness" meter and a "Skip for Now" option that leads to the Marketplace.Global Logout: A persistent "Logout" button that returns users to the initial Student/Landlord entry point.3. Student POV (Mobile-First Web UI)Marketplace (Home): Berkeley-themed dashboard. Top "Verified Student" badge. Horizontal scroll for "Recently Viewed." List housing cards with price, distance to campus, and a Gold Verified Badge.Property Detail Page: Hero image with a "Verified Landlord" trust row. Tabs for Overview, Amenities, and Verified Reviews (using @berkeley.edu truncated usernames).Application Tracker: Timeline view (Submitted → Viewed → Interview → Decision). Include a "Message Landlord" shortcut.Messaging Dashboard: Integrated DM interface with placeholders for "Start Video Call." Ensure threads are organized by property.Search/Filter: Fully functional filter for Price, Bed/Bath, and Lease Length (6-mo vs. 12-mo).4.
